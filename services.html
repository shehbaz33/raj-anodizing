Our Services at Raj Anodizing

Anodizing Works and Coating Solutions Tailored to Your Needs

Raj Anodizing Work offers a comprehensive range of services to meet your surface finishing needs. Our expertise includes:

Electro Colour Anodizing:

Enhance the appearance and durability of your products with our precision Electro Colour Anodizing process. This specialized surface treatment method not only adds vibrant colors to your materials but also creates a durable, corrosion-resistant layer. Our team of experts ensures that every anodized component meets the highest quality standards, resulting in products that not only look stunning but also stand the test of time.

Powder Coating

Experience the ultimate in finishing with our Powder Coating services. Our skilled technicians apply a fine, dry powder to your products, ensuring a sleek and uniform finish that's both aesthetically pleasing and highly durable. Powder coating offers exceptional resistance to chipping, fading, and wear and tear, making it an ideal choice for a wide range of applications. Trust us to deliver impeccable results that protect and beautify your items.

PU Coating

Elevate your products to a new level of excellence with our Polyurethane (PU) Coating solutions. PU coating offers the perfect combination of superior protection and aesthetics. It forms a robust, long-lasting barrier against environmental factors, while simultaneously enhancing the visual appeal of your items. Our PU coating process ensures a flawless finish that not only shields your products but also elevates their overall quality and appearance. Choose PU coating for uncompromising protection and style.
